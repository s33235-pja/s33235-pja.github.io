Petrichor V survival guide

Crash. Loot. Survive.

Risk of Rain 2 is a chaotic third-person roguelike where every minute makes the planet more dangerous. The player collects items, unlocks survivors, fights bosses and experiments with artifacts that completely change the rules of each run.

Why this game feels different

Endless scaling

The longer the run continues, the stronger enemies become. This creates constant pressure and makes every decision matter: farm for more items or rush the teleporter.

Crazy item builds

Items stack and combine. A simple character can become a screen-clearing machine after enough loot, especially when several effects trigger at the same time.

Survivor identity

Every survivor has a different rhythm: Commando is simple and flexible, Loader is aggressive and mobile, Artificer focuses on huge elemental bursts.

My run notes

Best beginner pick

Commando is the safest first choice because his skills are simple and items clearly show how power scaling works.

Most fun survivor

Loader feels the most dynamic for me: grappling around the map and hitting one strong punch makes the run very fast.

Build idea

Lens-Maker's Glasses, Soldier's Syringe and Paul's Goat Hoof are easy items to understand and combine well on many survivors.
