Items are the main way to become stronger in Risk of Rain 2. Every pickup changes your run: more damage, faster movement, healing, explosions, chain effects or dangerous corrupted powers.

Item collection

The cards below are styled like small collectible posters. Each item has a class, a rarity color and a short gameplay description.

Common / Damage

Lens-Maker's Glasses

Increases critical strike chance. A simple item that becomes very strong when stacked.

Common / Mobility

Paul's Goat Hoof

Increases movement speed, helping the survivor dodge attacks and reach the teleporter faster.

Common / Attack Speed

Soldier's Syringe

Raises attack speed. It is useful for almost every survivor because it improves damage output.

Common / Healing

Medkit

Gives healing after taking damage. It is a safe defensive choice during long fights.

Uncommon / Proc Damage

AtG Missile Mk. 1

Chance to fire a missile on hit. Great for chain damage and builds with many attacks.

Uncommon / Burst

Kjaro's Band

High damage hits create a fiery tornado. Best on survivors with powerful single attacks.

Uncommon / Mobility

Hopoo Feather

Adds an extra jump. It improves survival because vertical movement avoids many enemy attacks.

Uncommon / Equipment

Fuel Cell

Gives more equipment charges and lowers cooldowns, making active items much more important.

Legendary / Luck

57 Leaf Clover

Improves random chances. It makes many proc-based builds much stronger and more reliable.

Legendary / Chain Kill

Ceremonial Dagger

Killing enemies releases seeking daggers. Very powerful when many small enemies appear.

Legendary / Armor Break

Shattering Justice

Repeated hits reduce enemy armor, helping the team destroy bosses and tanky monsters.

Legendary / Cooldown

Alien Head

Reduces skill cooldowns. Strong on survivors that depend on abilities more than basic attacks.

Boss / Healing

Leeching Seed

Heals the survivor when dealing damage. Works well with fast attacks and multi-hit skills.

Boss / Summon

Queen's Gland

Summons a Beetle Guard ally. It distracts enemies and adds extra pressure in combat.

Void / Corrupted Damage

Plasma Shrimp

Corrupts AtG Missile. When you have shield, attacks fire small plasma missiles.

Lunar / Risk Reward

Gesture of the Drowned

Reduces equipment cooldown but activates equipment automatically. Powerful, but risky.

How to build

Build classes

Good runs usually mix several item classes instead of focusing on only one statistic.

Damage class

Critical chance, missiles, bands and explosions. This class clears enemies faster.

Mobility class

Movement speed and extra jumps. This class keeps the survivor alive by dodging.

Healing class

Medkits, seeds and defensive effects. This class makes mistakes less dangerous.

Utility class

Cooldown, equipment charges and special effects. This class changes how the run is played.

Beginner item classes

Damage

Items that increase direct damage or create extra attacks. They make bosses and elites die faster.

Mobility

Movement items help with dodging, reaching the teleporter and surviving late-game chaos.

Defense

Healing, shields and armor effects make mistakes less punishing, especially during long stages.
