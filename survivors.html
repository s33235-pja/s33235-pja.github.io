Balanced survivor

Commando

Commando is the most understandable survivor: mobile, reliable and focused on constant shooting. He is good for learning the game because his kit teaches positioning, cooldown control and item scaling.

Dual pistols Beginner friendly Consistent damage

Fast ranged survivor

Huntress

Huntress can attack while sprinting, so she feels very fluid. She works well with mobility and attack-speed items and is great for players who prefer dodging instead of standing still.

Auto-aim bow High mobility Glass-cannon style

Robot survivor

MUL-T

MUL-T is a heavy utility robot. His weapon swap makes him flexible, and his higher durability allows safer runs. He fits players who like experimenting with equipment and different attack patterns.

Tanky Weapon swap Utility

Elemental burst

Artificer

Artificer uses fire, ice and lightning to deal large bursts of damage. She rewards careful aim and timing, because her attacks are powerful but not as constant as automatic weapons.

Fire Ice wall Burst damage

High mobility brawler

Loader

Loader uses grappling and charged punches. She is one of the most aggressive survivors: fast, strong and able to cross the map quickly while smashing enemies at close range.

Grapple Melee power Burst mobility

Poison creature

Acrid

Acrid spreads poison and can weaken large groups over time. He feels different from weapon-based survivors because much of his damage comes from damage-over-time effects.

Poison Hybrid melee Crowd control
