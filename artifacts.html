Gameplay modifiers

Art ifacts

Artifacts change the rules of a run: item choice, enemy types, friendly fire, double spawns and other modifiers.

How to unlock them

1. Reach Sky Meadow

Find the Compound Generator on the fifth stage. It is the place where artifact codes are entered.

2. Enter symbols

Each artifact has a 3×3 symbol pattern. Use square, circle, triangle and diamond plates.

3. Complete the trial

The portal leads to Bulwark's Ambry. Defeat enemies, collect keys and destroy the Artifact Reliquary.
